About Me

Hi, I’m Andreas, the photographer behind Through The Lens. I’m passionate about capturing real moments and turning them into images that feel natural, expressive, and timeless.

From portraits and events to creative and automotive photography, my goal is to create photos that tell a story and preserve a feeling — not just an image.

I work closely with clients to understand their vision and deliver results that feel personal, unique, and true to the moment.
